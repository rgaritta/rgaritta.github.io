About

Traverser is a musical juxtaposition spreading themselves out in all the right directions. Bold, yet dynamic. Precise, yet cavernous. Crystal clean, yet atomically heavy. Armed with a technical prog exterior and guts so packed full of catchy hooks, thoughtful vocals, and harmonious melodies, that they remain elusive to any genres or clicks.

While it’s clear their attention to musicianship and songwriting craft is everything, their live performances are also a spectacle to behold -- boasting weighted, bombastic, and psychedelic fervor. Their upcoming record, "Jupiter Doesn’t Care About You," stands to be their most exhaustive and fully-realized work to date and is sure to lure you into the depths before releasing you back to the surface.
